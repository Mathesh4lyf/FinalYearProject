• PROMASIDOR LIMITED KENYA

    • About Promasidor Kenya Limited

    Our Mission

    To deliver innovative and delicious food solutions that cater to the diverse needs of Kenyan consumers, while promoting sustainable practices and ethical sourcing.

    Our Vision

    To be the leading provider of high-quality, nutritious, and affordable food products in Kenya, empowering communities and enriching lives.

    Our Core Values

    • Quality: We are committed to delivering products that meet the highest standards of safety, hygiene, and nutritional value.
    • Innovation: We continuously strive to develop new and exciting food products that cater to evolving consumer preferences.
    • Affordability: We believe everyone deserves access to nutritious food, and we make our products accessible to a wide range of consumers.
    • Sustainability: We are dedicated to operating in an environmentally responsible manner and minimizing our impact on the planet.
    • Ethical Sourcing: We source our ingredients responsibly and ethically, ensuring fair treatment of farmers and suppliers.
    • Community Support: We are committed to giving back to the communities we serve and fostering positive social impact.
    • Employee Well-being: We value our employees and strive to create a positive and rewarding work environment.
